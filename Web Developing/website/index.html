Founder

Akash Kumar is an emerging legal scholar and interdisciplinary researcher specializing in the intersection of law, forensic science, and technology. And also a Law Student at Jamia Hamdard

He is an IIT Kanpur certified professional and has completed the program “Mind, Machine & Justice: Legal Implications of Neurotechnology” by the National Forensic Science University (India) in collaboration with The Centre for Neurotechnology and Law (UK).

He is a published author in the Indian Journal of Law and Legal Studies, contributing to contemporary legal and forensic discourse.

His academic credentials include:
• Diploma in International Commercial Arbitration
• Certification in Sports Law in India (NLU Mumbai)
• Certification in Strategic Research & Drafting

He has been invited as a guest speaker at IIT Delhi, delivering sessions on ethical hacking, cybersecurity, and AI tools such as ChatGPT and DeepSeek.

Through Medico Legal Jurists, he aims to bridge the gap between legal theory and practical application.
